Ingredients (2 servings):
- 200g beef (thinly sliced)
- 1L beef broth
- 100g rice noodles
- 1 onion, 1 piece of ginger
- Spices: star anise, cinnamon stick, cloves
- Fish sauce, salt, sugar to taste
- Garnish: bean sprouts, lime, chili, fresh herbs

Steps:
1. Toast onion and ginger until slightly burnt, then add to beef broth with spices. Simmer 30 minutes.
2. Cook noodles separately, drain, and place in bowls.
3. Add raw beef slices on top.
4. Pour the hot broth over to cook the beef.
5. Serve with herbs, bean sprouts, lime, and chili.
