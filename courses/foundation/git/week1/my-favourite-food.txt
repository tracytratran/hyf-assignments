🇻🇳 Gỏi cuốn (Fresh Spring Rolls)
----------------------------------
Ingredients (for 6 rolls):
- 6 rice paper wrappers
- 6 cooked shrimp (cut in half)
- 1 cup cooked rice noodles
- 6 lettuce leaves
- Fresh herbs: mint, cilantro, basil
- ½ cucumber (cut into thin strips)

Steps:
1. Dip one rice paper in warm water for 5 seconds.
2. Lay it flat, add lettuce, herbs, cucumber, noodles, and shrimp.
3. Fold the sides and roll tightly.
4. Serve with peanut dipping sauce (mix peanut butter, hoisin sauce, and a bit of water).
