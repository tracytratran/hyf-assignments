Story of today

🫂 I received a cyber hug from 85-year-old Tony today 🫂

This morning marked the third time I received a call from the Be My Eyes app, and the second time I was able to answer. Within just eight months, I’ve already had three calls! My husband and a few friends even joked that I must be “the chosen one” (hehe maybe I’m just a little lucky).

Today’s call was from Tony, an 85-year-old gentleman who needed a quick hand. It was a small task for me, but the way he thanked me afterward with the warmest cyber hug made my heart melt. For a moment, I felt like a real-life super heroine who had just completed an important mission on Earth 🦸‍♀️

It’s amazing how such small moments of connection can fill my heart with so much happiness and gratitude. Each call leaves me smiling for the rest of the day and reminds me how technology, when used with empathy, can make the world a little kinder 🌍✨
